ICE-TCS -- Icelandic Centre of Excellence in Theoretical Computer Science

This is a stub for the new website of the ICE-TCS research center of the Department of Computer Science, Reykjavik University.

We are the theoretical computer science lab of the department.

We do combinatorics, algorithms and complexity, logic, concurrency theory, programming language theory.

The university suffered from a major cyberattack in early Feb. 2024. We have just regained access to our data. The new website will appear here.
