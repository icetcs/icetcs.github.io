The categoricity ordinal of a structure ℳ is a measure of how hard it is to compute isomorphisms between copies of ℳ. Presburger arithmetic is the theory of (ℤ, +, < ). We will precisely define categoricity ordinals, explore computability-theoretic properties of Presburger arithmetic, and examine which ordinals can be the categoricity ordinal for a model of Presburger arithmetic.
